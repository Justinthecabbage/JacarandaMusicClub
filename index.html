Welcome to Jacaranda

Jacaranda Music Club is named after the Jacaranda tree, which symbolizes youth, elegance, dreams and growth. The purple-blue blooming represents our passion for music and the beauty of teenage life.

Established on October 5, 2025, our club gathers students who love music including pop, classical, jazz, folk and vocal performance. We hold regular rehearsals, campus performances, music sharing activities and public welfare shows.

Our goal is to enrich campus cultural life, improve artistic literacy, and build a warm and professional music community for all young music lovers.
